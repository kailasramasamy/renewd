# Renewd — WhatsApp Launch Message

**How much have you lost to forgotten renewals?**

That Netflix charge you forgot to cancel. The gym membership you meant to pause. The SaaS tool you used once. They all quietly drain your money every month.

But it gets worse.

Forgot to renew your car insurance? You're driving uninsured — one accident and you're paying out of pocket. Missed your health insurance renewal? That hospital visit just became a financial nightmare. Let your domain or business license lapse? Your website goes down, your business is non-compliant, and competitors swoop in.

These aren't small inconveniences. They're show-stoppers.

**Renewd** fixes this. One app that:
- Tracks all your subscriptions and renewals in one place
- Reminds you before you get charged or before something lapses
- Shows you exactly where your money is going

No more surprise charges. No more costly lapses. No more "I forgot."
