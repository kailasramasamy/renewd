Delete from the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the app:

  1. Open Renewd and go to Profile
  2. Tap Delete Account
  3. Confirm deletion in the dialog
This action is permanent. Deleting your account will permanently remove all your data including renewals, documents, payments, reminders, notifications, and settings. This cannot be undone.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your user profile and account
  • All renewals and payment records
  • All uploaded documents
  • All reminders and notification history
  • All support tickets
  • All chat and AI usage history

Can't Access the App?

If you're unable to delete your account from the app, submit a request below and we'll process it within 3 business days.

Request received.
We'll process your account deletion within 3 business days and send a confirmation to your email.
